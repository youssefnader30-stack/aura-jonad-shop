# AuraJonad — Brand System

## Positioning
AI automation systems for real operators — not toys, not courses, but working machines.

## Taglines (ranked)
1. **Automate the boring. Scale the genius.** ← primary
2. Business systems that think.
3. AI that does the work. Not just talks about it.
4. Ship systems, not prompts.

## Voice
Direct · operator-to-operator · zero fluff · one claim, one proof · imperative mood.
Forbidden: "unlock", "leverage", "seamless", "empower", "transform your business" (all fired).

## Palette (dark-first, techy/futuristic)

| Role | Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| bg-base | Void | `#0A0A0B` | page background |
| bg-raise | Slate-950 | `#111114` | cards, sections |
| bg-line | Slate-800 | `#1F1F24` | borders |
| fg-primary | Ice | `#F5F7FA` | body text |
| fg-muted | Steel | `#8A8F98` | secondary text |
| accent | Aura Cyan | `#00E0D1` | CTAs, links |
| accent-glow | Plasma | `#6A5CFF` | gradients, highlight |
| signal | Amber | `#FFB020` | warnings, limited offers |
| danger | Crimson | `#FF4D6D` | errors, destructive |

**Gradient hero:** `linear-gradient(135deg, #00E0D1 0%, #6A5CFF 100%)`

## Typography
- **Display:** Geist Sans, 700 weight, -0.03em tracking
- **Body:** Inter, 400/500, -0.01em tracking
- **Mono (code/pricing):** JetBrains Mono, 500

## Logo
- Wordmark: "AURA·JONAD" in Geist 700, small dot separator
- Mark: overlapping cyan→violet ring with thin crosshair (see /brand/logo.svg)
- Minimum size: 24px mark, 80px wordmark
- Clear space: 0.5× mark height on all sides

## Imagery
- Dark UI screenshots with cyan glow
- Schematic/blueprint-style illustrations (not 3D renders)
- No stock photos of humans unless founder portrait
- System diagrams preferred over abstract tech clichés

## Do / Don't
| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| Show real workflow screenshots | Use "AI robot hand touching human finger" |
| Quote exact time savings ("14h/wk") | Vague ROI claims |
| Price on the page | "Contact for pricing" |
| One H1 per page | Multiple competing CTAs |
